Fried Eggs with Chilli Crisp

Gooey, runny but crispy fried egg are a thing of beauty. But how to take them to supermodel status? Drizzle them in chilli crisp, a winning concoction of spicy oil and crunchy bits of heaven. Anything goes with these eggs: serve them on top of fried rice and noodles, with your morning cooked breakfast, on top of avocado toast... the only thing you're limited by your imagination.

Preparation Time

  • Prep Time: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Serves: =2

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Vegetable Cooking Oil
  • Chilli Crisp

Steps

1.

In a medium frying pan, add oil to about 1cm up the sides of the pan. Place over a high heat. Once hot, carefully crack in the eggs.

2.

Leave to cook 2-3 minutes or until the edges have formed a crispy brown crust.

3.

Using a slotted spatula, transfer eggs to a serving plate. Liberally drizzle over the chilli crisp.
